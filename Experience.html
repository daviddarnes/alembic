    > 香港科技大学 （ 2016年9月 ~ 2021年7月 ）

    PhD，指导老师是Prof. Xian (sherry) CHEN以及Prof. Shengwang DU。我们研究组现在研究的方面有马氏体材料表面形貌的表征，微分干涉相差显微镜，马氏体相变，布拉格衍射，劳厄衍射，相场法，记忆金属以及马氏体相变材料制备等相关方向。以下是本人在香港科技大学期间的相关经历。

    显微镜实验平台搭建

    显微镜是观察马氏体相变的有效办法之一，比起原子力显微镜（AFM）可以看到材料相变的全貌，速度快，但并不能看到更微观的结构。我们研究组用的显微镜是Nikon Ni-U显微镜，并利用SDK对显微镜的照相机进行二次开发，提取原始的数据，并对原始的图片数据进行处理，以达到能用三维复原材料表面高度梯度的目的。除此以外，我们还自己研发了一套微分干涉相差显微镜用以更好更快的观察材料表面的三维形貌。

    X光衍射图谱模拟

    根据布拉格晶格衍射的原理，考虑结构因子，温度因子，衍射角度等因素，利用MATLAB便可以模拟出X光对某一特定晶体材料的衍射图谱。详情请参考XRD Simulation。

    > Hong Kong University of Science and Technology ( September, 2016 ~ July, 2021)

    I am a graduated PhD of HKUST and my supervisor is Prof. Xian (sherry) CHEN and Prof. Shengwang DU. Our research group interested in surface topography study of martensitic materials, differential interference constrast microscope, Martensite Phase Transformation，Bragg diffraction，Laue diffraction，Phase-field Model，Shape Memory Alloy andManufacturing Martensite materials. Here is my relevent experiences below.

    Building up the microscope system to observe the Martensite transformation

    Microscope is a efficient way to observe the Martensite transformation. It can catch the whole picture of the phase transformation quite fast. But it cannot reach the resolution as the AFM. Our research group is using Nikon's Ni-U microscope to take the picture of the Martensite phase transformation. We are using SDK package to develop the CCD, get the raw data and reconstruct the surface topography of materials. And we also build up a new differential interference contrast microscope system to characterize the surface topography of marterials.

    XRD simulation

    According to Bragg's law, considering the structure factor, temperature factor, diffraction angle and other factors, we can use MATLAB as our tool to code a program to simulate the XRD result of a particular crystal material. Please refer to XRD Simulation.
